Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Complete a degree in Business Administration or Management.
  2. Participate in internships to gain practical experience.
  3. Develop leadership and communication skills.
  4. Earn relevant certifications such as PMP or MBA.
  5. Build a strong professional network through events and LinkedIn.
  6. Start in an entry-level role and work your way up through promotions.
